Elevate Aura ("the App", "we", "our", "us") provides quiz-based

you agree to this Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information Provided by You

  • Name (Google Sign-In)
  • Email address (Google Sign-In)
  • Google profile picture (optional)

  ⚠️ We do NOT access your Gmail inbox, contacts, files, or 
     personal messages.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  Device Information:
    • Device model
    • Android version
    • Operating system details
    • App version

  Usage Data:
    • Quiz attempts, scores, correct/incorrect answers
    • Streaks, coins, badges, app interactions
    • Time spent in the app
    • Button clicks (non-personal behavior logs)

  Crash & Diagnostics:
    • Crash logs
    • Performance data
    • Error reports

  Approximate Location:
    • Automatically collected by advertising partners 
      (AdMob/Unity Ads)
    • NOT precise GPS location

1.3 Data Collected Through Third-Party Services

  Google Sign-In:
    • Name, email, profile image
    • Authentication token

  Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases):
    • Transaction ID and purchase status (We do NOT collect credit card details)

  Advertising Partners (AdMob / Unity Ads):
    • Device identifiers (AAID)
    • Approximate location
    • Ad interaction data

  Analytics & Backend (Supabase):
    • Session events, quiz progress, and wallet balances
    • User behavior summaries
    • Error tracking and performance logs
